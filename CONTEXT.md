# Personal Platform

The online home for Romain Lanz's public content, Courses, community spaces, and supporting site utilities.

## Language

### Platform

**Personal Platform**:
The online home for Romain Lanz's public content, Courses, community spaces, and supporting site utilities. It starts with Articles, but the language should leave room for Lessons, Courses, and possibly community discussion.
_Avoid_: Blog, portfolio, article site

### Publishing

**Article**:
A written piece published on the Personal Platform, usually attached to a category and intended to be read independently.
_Avoid_: Post, blog post, publication

**Category**:
The primary editorial grouping for Articles. An Article belongs to at most one Category, and a Category represents a stable topic area rather than a free-form label.
_Avoid_: Tag, taxonomy, topic

**Newsletter Subscriber**:
A person who asked to receive updates from the Personal Platform by email.
_Avoid_: Lead, contact, user

### Learning

**Course**:
A structured learning product made of Lessons, optionally organized into Chapters. A Course can be small and linear or large and chaptered, but it always provides a guided path through a topic.
_Avoid_: Formation, training, program, series

**Course Rating**:
A user evaluation of a Course as a whole. A Course Rating reflects the value of the complete learning path, not an individual Lesson.
_Avoid_: Lesson rating, review, score

**Chapter**:
An ordered section within a Course that groups related Lessons. Chapters exist only inside Courses, and a Lesson in a Course belongs to at most one Chapter.
_Avoid_: Module, section, part

**Lesson**:
A single learning unit that teaches one focused subject through one or more learning materials. A Lesson can belong to a Course or stand on its own.
_Avoid_: Episode, cours, standalone lesson

**Lesson Notes**:
The textual companion to a Lesson, summarizing what the Lesson teaches and giving learners a readable reference after or instead of watching.
_Avoid_: Article, summary article, transcript

**Video**:
A watchable learning material attached to a Lesson. A Video is one way a Lesson can teach its subject, but it is not the Lesson itself.
_Avoid_: Episode, recording, screencast

**Comment**:
A user contribution attached to a Lesson, used to ask questions, share feedback, or discuss the Lesson's subject.
_Avoid_: Reply, forum post, message

### Utilities

**Paste**:
A short text snippet shared through the Personal Platform for quick access or reference. A Paste is a utility item, not an Article or Lesson.
_Avoid_: Article, note, snippet

**Redirect**:
A short platform-owned path that sends visitors to another internal or external destination. Redirects are used to share stable or memorable links without exposing the final URL.
_Avoid_: Link, shortcut, alias, URL shortener

## Example Dialogue

**Dev**: Should this written recap be an Article?
**Domain**: No, if it exists because of a Lesson, it is Lesson Notes. Articles stand on their own.

**Dev**: What do we call a small Course without Chapters?
**Domain**: Still a Course. Chapters are optional; Series is not part of the language.

**Dev**: Can users discuss a Lesson?
**Domain**: Yes, through Comments attached to the Lesson. Future forum discussions should use their own language.

**Dev**: Is a Redirect only for external links?
**Domain**: No, a Redirect can send visitors to an internal or external destination.
